Novelty

Prenatal Care System is a device that ensures proper guidance for the mother and fetus remotely. The device is a belt worn by the mother on her abdomen which uses sensors to monitor the foetal and maternal heart rate. The belt not only measures the heart rates of mother and foetus but also the mother's blood sugar, pressure and body temperature in a non-invasive way.

Accuracy

Accurately monitor body conditions at any time by using this single device.

Classification

Application will classify the health status as Normal, Above average and Critical.

Emergency Service

In critical cases , emergency services from a nearby hospital is notified immediately.And if it's normal or above average the personal doc and loved ones are notified.

Cloud Storage

Data will be sent to the customised mobile application.The doctors can provide medicine prescription if any through the application.They can also schedule a personal meeting if required.
